Service Projects
Baton Rogue Flood Relief
On August 20-24, 2016, the Eta Zeta chapter traveled to Baton Rogue to help remodel houses and help families in need form the flooding.
Northeast Louisiana Food Drive
On the week of February 18, 2017, the Eta Zeta chapter participated in the NorthEast Louisiana Food drive. Members were required to bring 5 canned vegetables or other goods to donate to families that needed them.


Philanthropy Events
Food Relief GoFundMe Page
On the week of August 1, 2016, the Eta Zeta chapter set up a Go-Fund-Me page and raised a total of $3000 to donate to victims of the Baton Rogue floods.
Up Til Dawn
On November 1, 2016, the Eta Zeta chapter participated in the St. Jude Children's Research Hospital fundraiser called "Up Til Dawn". Several members set up donation pages, and raised a total of $500.
Car Wash
On April 1, 2017, the Eta Zeta chapter hosted a car wash open to the community in the parking lot of the Fraternity House. A total amount of $413 was raised, and all proceeds went to MedCamps of Louisiana.
Golf Tournament
On May 5, 2017, the Eta Zeta chapter hosted it's first annual "St Judes Golf Tournament". All members were required to sell two tickets for participants in the tournament. This fundraiser was alot of fun, and a huge sucess. In total, we raised nearly $7000, all of which went to St. Judes.


Chapter Parcipitation
Dog Haul
On September 6, 2016 the Eta Zeta chapter participated in Dog Haul, an event put on by Louisiana Tech to give assistance to new Freshmen moving in the apartments and dorms.
Moving elderly Community Member into New Home
On September 9, 2016, a few members helped move in an elderly lady into her new home since she could not do it on her own.
Trunk or Treat
On October 26, 2016, Members helped set up a truck for kids to trick or treat and and also helped with events they had going on for the kids at the local Trinity Church.
Squire Creek Gala
On November 5, 2016, a few members volunteered to help with a fundraiser at Squire Creek Country Club
Clearing New Bike/Running Trail at Cook Park
On the week of Feburary 5,2017, several members helped clear out and form a bike/running trail for community members to exercise and be outdoors more.
Big Event
On March 25, 2017, almost every Member volunteered to help Ruston Community Members do yard work or whatever the host needed them to do around the city of Ruston.
Peachy Keen Caper
On April 2, 2016, several members helped put on the largest fundraiser the "Big Brother/Big Sister of Northeast Louisiana Foundation" does every year at Squire Creek Country Club.
